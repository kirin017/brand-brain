## What To Avoid

- Cam kết giảm cân
- Detox/thải độc thần kỳ
- Hứa hẹn thu nhập
- Khan hiếm/khẩn cấp giả

## When To Hand Off To Sale/Admin

- Customer asks for price/order.
- Customer asks about disease, pregnancy, children, medication, or special diet.
- Customer asks for membership price/benefit not yet confirmed.
- CTV/Leader asks about commission or policy.
- Partner asks to use BYT logo, QR, or post deal.
