## Typography Direction

- Official font: `Cần founder xác nhận`.

## Required Elements

- Clear healthy F&B context.
- Product/membership cue if visually confirmed.
- Space for headline and CTA.
- Natural lifestyle context relevant to audience.
- Founder confirmation note in handoff, not necessarily inside final image.

## Forbidden Elements

- BYT logo if official logo placement is not provided.
- Fake product packaging.
- QR codes.
- Medical/hospital style.
- Doctors, pills, stethoscope, clinic setting.
- Extreme gym/fitness/body transformation.
- Before/after body imagery.
- Fake luxury.
- Neon colors.
- Generic stock-photo feeling.
- Overcrowded text.
- Any visual implying disease cure, detox miracle or guaranteed weight loss.

## Compliance Notes

- No disease treatment, prevention or cure.
- No guaranteed weight loss.
- No miracle detox or thải độc visual metaphor.
- No disease-specific visual claims about diabetes, blood fat, liver, insomnia, digestion or immunity.
- No income/profit promise visuals for CTV/Sale/Leader/connected point.
- If children, pregnant women, patients or medication appear, human approval is required.
