## Visual Style

- Warm, natural, clean, trustworthy.
- F&B and community-first.
- Practical for Vietnamese families and busy women.
- Product and CTA clarity before decoration.

## Colors

- Use color tokens only if confirmed.
- If brand colors are missing, create neutral placeholder styles and label them `Cần founder xác nhận`.
- Do not invent final HEX colors.
- Avoid neon, fake luxury and overly clinical palettes.

## Typography Direction

- Use readable sans-serif hierarchy.
- H1/headline must be short and mobile-readable.
- Supporting text must not crowd the frame.
- Official font: `Cần founder xác nhận`.

## Required Elements

- BYT brand name or approved logo placeholder.
- Product/membership name.
- Main message.
- CTA.
- Channel-specific element:
  - Zalo: group/community cue.
  - POSM/QR card: QR placeholder and short CTA.
  - Sale kit: checklist/table format.
  - Partner proposal: partner value and scope.
- Founder confirmation markers for missing assets.

## Forbidden Elements

- Hospital/medical style.
- Extreme fitness/gym style.
- Fake luxury.
- Neon colors.
- Generic stock-photo layout.
- Too much text.
- Unapproved BYT logo, QR, packaging or partner logo.
- Claim-heavy health badges.

## Compliance Notes

- No medical, disease treatment or disease prevention claim.
- No guaranteed weight loss.
- No miracle detox/thải độc.
- No unsupported claims about diabetes, blood fat, liver, insomnia, digestion or immunity.
- No income, revenue or profit promise.
- If information is missing, mark `Cần founder xác nhận`.

## Figma Handoff Requirements

- Name frames clearly.
- Use editable text layers.
- Use reusable components for CTA, QR placeholder, product badge and compliance note.
- Keep spacing consistent.
- Add a hidden or side note listing founder confirmations.
- Do not export final publishing asset until Brand Checker and human approval pass.
