# Sample Image Generation Prompt - BYT Zalo Banner Background

## Tool

- Target tool: Image generation tool
- Image purpose: Background/mood image for Zalo group banner
- Final publishing image: No, requires human approval
- Aspect ratio: Zalo banner ratio, exact size cần designer xác nhận
- Resolution: High enough for Canva/Figma layout

## Brand Context

Bếp Yêu Thương / BYT is a Vietnamese healthy F&B brand, proactive wellness community, membership-based model and B2B2C ecosystem. BYT helps Vietnamese families and busy women start small healthy habits through food, drinks, membership, Zalo community and connected local touchpoints.

Brand essence:

```text
Ăn lành. Uống sạch. Sống yêu thương.
```

The image should feel warm, natural, trustworthy, caring, practical and community-oriented.

## Audience

- Audience: Thành viên nhóm Zalo ăn lành / sống khỏe chủ động.
- Audience context: Người bận rộn muốn có nhắc nhở nhẹ nhàng và lựa chọn ăn/uống lành hơn.
- Emotional need: Cảm giác được đồng hành, không bị phán xét.

## Product / Membership

- Product/membership: Ban Mai Thức Giấc hoặc set ăn sáng healthy.
- Product role: Gợi ý thói quen bữa sáng nhẹ nhàng hơn.
- Confirmed visual assets available: Chưa rõ.
- Unconfirmed items: `Cần founder xác nhận`.

## Channel And Format

- Channel: Zalo group.
- Format: Zalo banner.
- Use case: Banner mở đầu tuần cho nhóm nuôi thói quen.

## Main Message

```text
Bắt đầu ngày mới bằng một lựa chọn bữa sáng lành hơn, gọn hơn và dễ duy trì hơn.
```

## Prompt Draft

```text
Create a warm, natural, trustworthy Vietnamese healthy F&B lifestyle background image for Bếp Yêu Thương (BYT), a proactive wellness community and membership-based B2B2C ecosystem.

Audience: busy Vietnamese women and Zalo community members who want small, practical healthy habits.
Product/membership: Ban Mai Thức Giấc / healthy breakfast habit. Do not show official packaging unless provided. Product visuals are placeholders and Cần founder xác nhận.
Channel/format: Zalo group banner.
Main message: start the day with a healthier, simpler breakfast choice that is easier to maintain.

Scene: a clean Vietnamese kitchen or small office breakfast table in soft morning light, with a simple healthy breakfast setup, a glass/bottle placeholder, fresh fruit or oats, and a calm community feeling. The scene should feel real, practical and caring, not staged luxury.

Style: warm natural light, clean composition, trustworthy healthy F&B mood, close to Vietnamese families and busy office workers.
Layout: leave clear negative space on the left or center for a short headline and CTA overlay; prioritize mobile readability; no crowded background.
Colors: warm natural clean palette; exact BYT brand colors are Cần founder xác nhận.
Typography: do not generate final text inside the image; official BYT fonts are Cần founder xác nhận.

Required: healthy breakfast context, natural real-life mood, space for text, product clarity if product asset is provided later.
Avoid: hospital style, doctors, medicine, medical cross, clinic setting, extreme fitness/gym, body transformation, fake luxury, neon colors, generic stock-photo feeling, too much text, fake BYT logo, fake QR, fake packaging, detox/thải độc or weight-loss miracle cues.
Compliance: no medical claims, no disease claims, no guaranteed weight loss, no miracle detox, no income promise.
```
