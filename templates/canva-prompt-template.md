- Do not invent final brand HEX colors.

## Typography Direction

- Use clear, friendly, readable sans-serif typography.
- Avoid overly decorative fonts.
- Do not invent official BYT fonts.
- If exact fonts are needed, write: `Cần founder xác nhận`.

## Required Elements

- BYT brand name or approved logo placeholder.
- Product/membership name.
- Main message.
- CTA.
- QR placeholder if required.
- Founder confirmation note for unconfirmed logo/color/font/QR/price/benefit.

## Forbidden Elements

- Hospital or medical style.
- Doctors, medicine, medical cross or treatment imagery.
- Extreme fitness/gym style.
- Body transformation or before/after.
- Fake luxury.
- Neon colors.
- Generic stock-photo feeling.
- Too much text.
- Unconfirmed logo, QR, packaging, color, font or partner logo as if official.

## Compliance Notes

- Do not claim disease treatment, prevention or cure.
- Do not claim guaranteed weight loss.
- Do not use miracle detox or thải độc language.
- Do not claim diabetes, blood fat, liver, insomnia, digestion or immunity benefits unless approved by human/compliance.
- Do not promise income, revenue or profit for CTV/Sale/Leader/connected point.
- Safer wording: hỗ trợ lối sống lành mạnh, giúp dễ bắt đầu thói quen healthy hơn, đồng hành chăm sóc chủ động.

## Canva Output Requirements

- Create an editable Canva layout.
- Keep text in editable layers.
- Keep product image/logo/QR as placeholders if assets are missing.
- Include a small internal note area or handoff note listing `Cần founder xác nhận`.
- Do not create final publishing asset until Brand Checker and human approval pass.
